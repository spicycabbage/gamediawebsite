About This Game

Castle Siege is an epic tower defense game set in a medieval fantasy world. Build and upgrade your defenses, position your towers strategically, and lead your troops to victory against increasingly challenging enemy hordes.

Key Features:

  • 200+ unique levels with different terrain and challenges
  • 15+ different tower types with unique abilities
  • Strategic resource management system
  • Hero units with special powers
  • Daily challenges and seasonal events
  • Offline campaign mode
  • Multiplayer co-op mode

Tower Types:

  • Archer Towers: Fast-firing ranged attacks
  • Cannon Towers: High damage area attacks
  • Magic Towers: Special effects and crowd control
  • Traps: Passive defensive structures
