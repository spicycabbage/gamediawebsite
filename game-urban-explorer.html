About This Game

Urban Explorer is an immersive spot the difference game that takes you on a journey through the world's most beautiful cities. Challenge yourself with increasingly difficult puzzles featuring stunning urban landscapes, famous landmarks, and hidden details.

Features:

  • 150+ carefully crafted levels
  • Beautiful HD graphics of real cities
  • Progressive difficulty curve
  • Hint system for challenging puzzles
  • Achievement system
  • Offline gameplay

Game Modes:

  • Classic Mode: Traditional spot the difference gameplay
  • Time Attack: Race against the clock
  • Zen Mode: Relaxed gameplay without time pressure
